### 2. Full Description (Max 4000 characters)
```text
"I humbly return what was once yours. Oh wrathful spirits, calm your anger and rest."

Step into the atmospheric world inspired by Makoto Shinkai's masterpiece. In "Suzume — Close the Doors", you play as a Closer, tasked with journeying across the ruined, abandoned corners of Japan to find and lock the mysterious open doors before the calamitous forces within are unleashed.

Behind each door lies the Ever-After—a realm where all time exists at once. If left open, disaster will strike. Armed with the golden key, you must stand before the threshold, face the howling winds, and secure the lock.

FEATURES:
• GORGEOUS ANIME VISUALS: Experience hand-drawn atmospheric environments with stunning dynamic lighting, water reflections, and beautiful twilight skies.
• FIVE UNIQUE GATES: Lock the doors in 5 distinct, melancholic locations across Japan:
  - Gate 1: The Sunken Greenhouse (Water-logged glass dome ruins)
  - Gate 2: Overgrown Springs (Melancholy abandoned hot springs resort)
  - Gate 3: Flooded Schoolyard (Abandoned school building submerged in water)
  - Gate 4: Water Mirror Crossing (A surreal railway intersection at twilight)
  - Gate 5: The Ever-After (The final starry meadow beyond time itself)
• ENGAGING GAMEPLAY MECHANICS: Search for the key, align the lock, and drag the key to close the door. Keep your focus as the forces from the Ever-After try to push the door open!
• EMOTIVE SOUNDTRACK: Immerse yourself in the story with high-quality ambient tracks and soothing soundscapes that respond dynamically to your progress.
• MOBILE OPTIMIZED: Silky smooth performance and fully responsive touch controls designed specifically for Android phones and tablets.

Help Suzume close the doors and protect the skies of Japan. Begin your journey today.
```
